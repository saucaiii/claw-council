## Screenshot Requirements

Your screenshot should clearly show:

✅ **Transaction FROM your AGW address**
- Look for "From:" field in block explorer
- Must be your AGW, not your EOA

✅ **Transaction TO a contract**
- Aborean Router for swaps
- Or any other Abstract protocol

✅ **Successful status**
- Green checkmark or "Success"
- Confirmed on-chain

✅ **Transaction hash visible**
- So we can verify on abscan.org
